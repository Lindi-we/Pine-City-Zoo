TIGER

There are tigers with distinct colors. Most tigers have orange fur with black stripes, and a white belly. The black stripes usually extend to the white underside.

Tigers can live in a variety of habitats. Mostly they need to hide, to be near a water jungle source, and have enough prey to eat.
